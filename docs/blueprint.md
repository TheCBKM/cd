# **App Name**: Chaitanya Organics

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog: Showcase a range of organic and eco-friendly products, including herbal powders, smoothies, and cow dung products, with detailed descriptions and images.
- Farm to Product Journey: Visually present the organic farming process at Chaitanyadham, from growing to packaging, emphasizing purity and nutritional value.
- Wellness Guide: Provide usage guidelines for herbal powders and smoothies, along with health benefits and disclaimers.
- Contact and Visit Information: Display contact details, including phone/WhatsApp number, email, and address, with information on visiting Chaitanyadham.
- Team Introduction: Introduce the team members with their backgrounds and roles in Chaitanya Enterprises.
- AI-Powered Health Suggestion Tool: An AI tool analyzes user-input health needs and recommends suitable products from the Chaitanya Organics catalog, acting as an informational assistant to the user. This tool will consider existing stock, popularity, and user feedback.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color (text): Dark green (#13631c) to reflect organic and healthy living.
- Background color: Off-white (#F5F5DC) to give a clean and earthy feel.
- Accent color (doli): Light brown (#c26608) for important elements to convey stability and naturalism.
- Accent color (veggies): Light green (#479a16) for representing natural produce.
- Headline font: 'Alegreya', a serif font, for a feeling of warmth and heritage
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a sans-serif font, for readability and a modern feel.
- Use hand-drawn style icons for products and features to convey authenticity.
- Use a modern, clean layout optimized for mobile devices, incorporating a grid-based structure with ample whitespace for readability. Employ a parallax scrolling effect to add depth and visual interest to the site.
- Subtle transitions and animations for page loading and product details, creating a smooth and engaging user experience. Incorporate tasteful animations on scroll to reveal content and guide the user through the site.
